I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong, China. My research interests include data mining, large language models, recommender systems, adversarial attack, domain adaptation, and pattern recognition. I received the Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi’an, China, in 2020 and 2023, respectively.
